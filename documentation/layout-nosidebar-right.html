Remove sidebar right

You can remove the Right Sidebar element directly from the html structure (bt-right-sidebar), you'll find it inside the main wapper, also you should remove the sidebar toggle button (bt-toggle-right-sidebar) from the top header, once they are removed your main structure should look like this:

<html lang="en">
<body>
  <div class="bt-wrapper">
    <nav class="navbar navbar-expand fixed-top bt-top-header">
      <!-- Top bar without sidebar toggle button -->
    </nav>
    <div class="bt-left-sidebar">
      <!-- Left sidebar -->
    </div>
    <div class="bt-content">
      <!-- Main content -->
    </div>
  </div> 
</body>
</html>
